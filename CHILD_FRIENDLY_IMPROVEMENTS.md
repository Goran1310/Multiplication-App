## ðŸŽ¨ Design Principles Applied

### 1. **Larger Touch Targets**
- All buttons minimum 55-60px height
- Wider padding for easier clicking

### 2. **Bigger Text**

- Increased font-weight from 600 to 700-800

### 5. **Enhanced Visual Feedback**
- Larger shadows for depth perception
- Hover effects with transforms

- Increased padding throughout (2.5rem containers)

- Maintained gradient backgrounds (engaging)
- High contrast for readability
- Different color themes per app for variety
